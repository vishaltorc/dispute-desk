dispute-desk

An open-source, AI-readable knowledge repository for Indian bank disputes.
Phase 1: HDFC Bank credit cards — first complaint through the bank's internal escalation.

This is an organisational and procedural tool, not legal advice, and it never predicts dispute outcomes. Verify every regulatory citation against the linked primary sources and every contact against the bank's official page before relying on them. Full disclaimer.

Use it with your AI assistant (the whole point)

Copy this message into ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, or any AI chat, and add your situation at the end:

Fetch https://vishaltorc.github.io/dispute-desk/ai-bundle.md — it is a knowledge
base for Indian bank credit card disputes. Follow the instructions in its
HOW_AI_SHOULD_USE_THIS section, then help me with my dispute. My situation: …

If your AI cannot fetch links (some consumer tiers can't), download the bundle file and attach it to the chat instead — it is the entire repository in one file.

What's inside

  • A precondition-gated escalation procedure (no skipped rungs, no premature filings)
  • Seven fill-in templates with placeholders — your data never leaves your machine
  • RBI and card-network rules, each linked to its official source with a last-verified date
  • HDFC escalation contacts captured from official pages, dated and source-linked
  • A catalogue of known deflection replies and their factual counters
Privacy: never commit your own statements or evidence to this repository or a fork. Templates use placeholders so real data stays with you.
Scope: RBI Ombudsman filing is deliberately out of scope in v1. Contacts are perishable — check the dates.
